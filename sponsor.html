So you'd like to sponsor Hack && Tell?

You're awesome.

What you need to know:

  • In 2018, our out-of-pocket costs averaged $200 per meetup.
  • We are completely volenteer based, and the organizers cover any shortfall in our budget.
  • This cost included fees from meetup.com and food and drink for the attendees.
  • Venmo is the preferred means of payment, but exceptions can be made for larger sponsorships.

Here's what you get out of the deal:

  • A chance to include a block of promotional text in our 2,000 member mailing list.
  • A shoutout at the start of the meetup, and if a representative attends, a few minutes to talk about your company.
  • Recognition from the Hacker and Maker community in DC.
  • Grow and support local artists and creatives in the area.
